Teacher and Full-Stack Developer

Hello! How are you? My name is Gabriel Antonio, and I am passionate about technology and people. Since I was young, I have always been curious about how things work — taking electronics apart, exploring software, and learning everything autodidactically. This curiosity grew over time and became a solid career in Information Technology.

My first job dealing directly with the public helped me develop clear and empathetic communication — a skill I carry with me today. When I started studying IT, I realized I could combine my two passions: interacting with people and technology. I found purpose in understanding clients' needs and transforming challenges into technological solutions.

Over time, I discovered another vocation: teaching. Sharing knowledge became one of my greatest achievements, and each new experience reinforces my love for learning and sharing what I know. I am driven by challenges, continuous learning, and the desire to grow every day — as a professional and as a person.
